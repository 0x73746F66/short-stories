# The Marauder's Archives

### *Untold Tales from the Wizarding World*

Welcome, fellow witches and wizards, to **The Marauder's Archives** — a sanctuary of magical stories hidden within the ancient halls of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Within these enchanted pages, you shall discover forgotten moments frozen in time, untold adventures etched in invisible ink, and the hidden stories of those brave souls who walked the castle's corridors long before Harry Potter's legendary journey began. Each tale has been carefully preserved, much like memories in a Pensieve, waiting for curious minds to uncover their secrets.

## Featured Story

### [The Sorting of Severus Snape](stories/snape-sorting-day.md)

*September 1st, 1971 — The Great Hall*

Before he became the enigmatic Potions Master dwelling in the dungeons, before his fateful friendship with Lily Evans was shattered beyond Reparo, Severus Snape was merely an eleven-year-old boy with greasy hair, a threadbare coat, and a heart full of desperate hope. This is the untold story of his first day at Hogwarts — the day the ancient Sorting Hat peered deep into his tormented soul and made a choice that would echo through the decades, shaping destinies and determining the fate of the wizarding world itself.

**Genre:** Origin Story | **Era:** Marauders' Era | **Character:** Severus Snape

## About This Archive

This fan site celebrates the rich and wondrous tapestry of J.K. Rowling's wizarding world through creative storytelling and imaginative exploration. All stories contained within are works of fan fiction, lovingly crafted with deep respect and admiration for the original Harry Potter series that has enchanted millions of readers worldwide.

> *"Words are, in my not-so-humble opinion, our most inexhaustible source of magic, capable of both inflicting injury and remedying it."*
> 
> — Albus Dumbledore

These archives stand as a testament to the enduring power of stories — how they inspire, transform, and unite us across generations and continents. Whether you're a Gryffindor at heart, possess the ambition of Slytherin, value Ravenclaw's wisdom, or embody Hufflepuff's loyalty, you'll find tales here that speak to the magic within us all.
